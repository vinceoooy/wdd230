W01 Project Introduction: The Chamber Site Plan

Site Name: Butuan Chamber of Commerce

A chamber of commerce in the locale of Butuan City, Philippines

Site Purpose

Butuan Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit organization that aims to increase awareness of local members and entrepreneurs on current developments affecting business to help them shape their respective work programs and maximize opportunities for developing and expanding their services through collaboration with local and international partners.

Butuan Chamber of Commerce also aims to provide all information needed for not just members but also for consumers/customers such as company or business details and exclusive vouchers or discounts available

Scenarios

-What are the current events or program that will aid my business to connect more to consumers?

-What programs or other business that I can make a network to have mutual benefits?

-What are the steps and processes to start a local and legal business?
